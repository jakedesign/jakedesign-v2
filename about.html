About Me

Hello, my name is Jake Armstead.

Since the day I was born, I've had a deep insatiable desire to create no matter the medium. I have found many outlets to channel this creativity, but the one thing that intrigues me the most is creating digital design experiences through the art of design and code!

When I’m not heads down at work, you’ll catch me working on music production, strolling around Portland getting my tastebuds satisfied with delicious food, hiking somewhere in the beautiful PNW, reading a good book at a coffee shop sippin on oat milk lattes, or re-watching the office for the 100th time!
